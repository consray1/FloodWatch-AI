# FloodWatch AI

## Project Summary

FloodWatch AI is a community-powered flood intelligence platform designed to complement HUSIKA and ICPAC hazard monitoring systems.

The platform enables citizens, community leaders, NGOs, emergency responders, and government agencies to report and verify flood-related incidents in real time.

The goal is to transform unstructured community reports into actionable intelligence that improves disaster response.

---

## Core Problem

Current flood early warning systems answer:

"Will flooding happen?"

They do not adequately answer:

* Where is flooding occurring now?
* Which roads are blocked?
* Which bridges are damaged?
* Which communities require evacuation?
* Where should responders be deployed?

FloodWatch AI fills this operational intelligence gap.

---

## Primary Objectives

1. Collect community reports
2. Analyze reports using AI
3. Geolocate incidents
4. Aggregate duplicate reports
5. Calculate risk scores
6. Visualize incidents on maps
7. Generate actionable alerts
8. Support emergency response operations
